Projects

This is a showcase of all projects, completed and in development.

Bed Engine

Bed Engine is an ECS based game engine that takes ECS's core fundimentals to its heart.

Mattress

Mattress is a physics engine that is a part of Bed Engine.

Pillow

Pillow is a Math library that is in the works for Bed Engine.

Wild Magic

You are a lone travelling wizard, traversing across a vast world of what was once a vibrant and lost civilization. However, it is inhabited by monstrous and hostile slimes who halt your path. What should you do? Take supplies, destroy the slimes or better yet... survive!

Tempus Rattus

Tempus Rattus is a game set in 1369 England, where you play as a plague infested rat on a mission to spread the black death to unsuspecting townsfolk.

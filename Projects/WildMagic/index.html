Wild Magic

Team 29 and part of a Collaborative Game Development module at Staffordshire University.

Description

You are a lone travelling wizard, traversing across a vast world of what was once a vibrant and lost civilization. However, it is inhabited by monstrous and hostile slimes who halt your path.

What should you do? Take supplies, destroy the slimes or better yet... survive!

Technologies Used

Unreal Engine 5 C++ Blueprints
